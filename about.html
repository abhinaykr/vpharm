About Us

Vibrant Pharma is a growth oriented contract research organization offering challenging synthetic organic chemistry projects in partnership with global pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies. We provide our customers with valuable chemistry solutions ranging from medicinal chemistry libraries to process development. In particular, large scale synthesis of advanced level intermediates, active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs), reference standards, stable isotopes and chiral ligands.
